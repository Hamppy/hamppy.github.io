Seasonal / Event Advertising

Campaign-specific theme visualizations for HOK-Elanto Restaurants

HOK-Elanto Restaurants have a rich tradition of organizing seasonal events that celebrate various cultures, culinary arts, and community engagements. Over the years, these events have become somewhat anticipated highlights, offering unique experiences to owner customers across various periods through the year. The concepts and schemas have affiliated a mixed media including the conventional point-of-purchase / point-of-sale prints, display advertising crop frames, social media publications and digital signage reels etc.

Copywriters: Jan-Erik Ehrström, Katri Karsi, Saara Kullström-Koljonen & Arja Luopa
Client: HOK-Elanto Restaurants with various cooperation partners.
